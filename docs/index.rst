What is Family Tree DNA?
========================

Family Tree DNA is a genetic testing company that provides DNA tests for genealogy and family history research. Founded in 2000, the company offers several testing options, including:

* Family Finder (Autosomal DNA)
* Y-DNA Testing
* Mitochondrial DNA (mtDNA)
* Ancient Origins Reports

After purchasing a DNA kit and submitting your sample, your results become available through your online Family Tree DNA account.

Why You Need to Log In
======================

Logging into your Family Tree DNA account allows you to:

* View DNA test results.
* Discover genetic matches.
* Build and manage your family tree.
* Explore ethnicity estimates.
* Download raw DNA data.
* Manage account settings.
* Order additional DNA tests.
* Participate in genealogy projects.

Without logging in, your personal DNA information remains inaccessible.

How to Access the Family Tree DNA Login Page
============================================

Follow these simple steps:

1. Open your preferred web browser.
2. Visit the official Family Tree DNA website.
3. Click the **Login** button located in the upper-right corner.
4. Enter your registered email address or kit number.
5. Type your password.
6. Click **Sign In**.

If your login details are correct, you'll be redirected to your personal dashboard.

Creating a New Family Tree DNA Account
======================================

If you have recently purchased a DNA kit, you'll need to activate it before accessing your account.

Steps include:

* Visit the official website.
* Click **Activate Kit**.
* Enter your kit number.
* Create a secure password.
* Verify your email address.
* Complete your profile information.

Once activation is complete, you can log in anytime using your credentials.

Forgot Your Password?
=====================

If you cannot remember your password:

1. Open the login page.
2. Select **Forgot Password**.
3. Enter your registered email address.
4. Check your email inbox.
5. Click the password reset link.
6. Create a new password.
7. Log in using your updated credentials.

Always choose a strong password containing:

* Uppercase letters
* Lowercase letters
* Numbers
* Special characters

Incorrect Password
------------------

--------------

Internet Connection
-------------------

Server Maintenance
------------------

Occasionally, Family Tree DNA performs scheduled maintenance that may temporarily affect login availability.
